Mission Statement

The Petite Treats website will be used as a new marketing medium to attract younger customers that use the web, this will increase revenue and customer base and keep these clients informed with the latest products and promotions.

Goal

The goal of this website is to establish a new younger clientbase and to provide these clients with an attractive and simple yet informative page showcasing current products on sale.

Success Evaluation

Success of this website will be evaluated based on the sales and foot traffic logged before and after the website was established and then compared.

Target Audience

The site is to attract young families and people between the images of 17 - 35 to the bakery.
